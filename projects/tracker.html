Practice Tracker

Practice Tracker is a web app designed for coaches and other group leaders in order to help them keep track of member attendence and training progress. It uses django and python as a back end and its features include creating groups, adding members, and adding practices to track. The app keeps track of member statistics and presents them in easy-to-read graphs.
